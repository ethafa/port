Paris en Liberté

Microtypography study for a promotional poster



Role Concepet development,
Visual Design, Prototyping
Course Typography
Supervisors Alessandra Dal Ben

After a research on the phootgrapher Robert Doisneau the brief was to create an poster for a exhibition about the photographer. To realize this poster was used a rigid sistem of grids and proportion.

The poster is made to be folded in four. The most interesting part is the bibliography in the back. The typography is used to create a dinamic outcome.
