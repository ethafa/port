The Hub

Advanced Environments and Communication
materials for a co-working in Lugano



Role Concepet development, Research, Design, Prototyping
Teammates Gianfranco Bertelli, Nydia Chavarria, Paolo Crocé Supervisors Fabio Franchino
Alice Mela
Course Designing Advanced Environments Committent DAGORÀ SA

The brief was to think about possible space interventions inside a new co-working space in Lugano. The purpose was to create installations inside the building that would offer a unique experience to the people inside,, help the networking within them and also present this co-working as a space where innovation can grow. After researching we found that in order to create a community, meaningful relationships are needed and they generally start in an informal context.

So the core of the project was develop for the common are of the space. We started from creating a new brand with values such as: “Belong, Inspire, Connect, Impact” and we call this new project “The-Hub”.
The-Hub / Innovation Starts Here. The-Hub is a cowork focused on innovation. The-Hub’s People are looking to Belong and Connect to an Inspiring community Who’s projects will Impact the society of tomorrow.

Brand
The logo is a pentagon with rounded angles representing the 5 industries inside the building (Design, Travel, Healthcare, Fashion and Food). The fill is a green gradient, the green is a remark of the logo of DAGORA the company that initiate the project. The choose of gradient wants to represent the aim of creating knowledge contamination between the the different


industries present in the building and among the people of the community. In future developments, If the client decides to open The-Hub in different locations the idea is that the logo will change adding the shortcut name for the city
(es. ge-Hub / zh-Hub / lu-Hub).


Project: Event Wall
Event wall is an installation meant to be used during events. It consist in a series of screens displaying a selection of projects (one for each industry) developed inside The-Hub. The informations about each project will consist in: a brief video at the beginning, a description highlighting the innovation aspect of the project and a feature that allow to receive further informations about the project or contact the authors to start collaborate or to invest.
