The Invisible Guilty

The Invisible Guilty is a web platform created with the aim of informing and sensitize readers towards a very important issue that afflicts the world today.



Role Concepet development,
Visual Design, Prototyping
Teammates Paolo Crocé
Course Web Platform for Interaction Designers Supervisors Fabio Franchino

The Invisible Guilty is a web platform created with the aim of informing and sensitize readers towards a very important issue that afflicts the world today: the impact of Methane emission on the climate warming.

The project is called “The invisible guilty” has we are speaking about the greenhouse gas Methane more dangerous than the CO2 and which is has suggested from the title hard to find.

Interface and Interaction modality
The reader will follow a guided path within the web platform, where he will be able to read and interact with some elements inside the page, the interactive parts, mainly mouse clicks and mouse-over are highlighted by the use of the green color. The primary purpose of these interactive elements is to provide additional information through the appearance of pop ups and side panels.The entire narrative path is divided into three main parts: at the beginning a humorous section dedicated to methane gas emitted by cows with related information to curiosity about it; Secondly a more serious part dedicated to methane gas in itself and to the damage it causes to our planet; a third and last part is dedicated to transmitting a message and a call to action towards the reader, where he will be asked to share what he has just learned about the issues.

The article present a number of floating green circles. Whenever a new reader decides to press the share button, a new circle will appear in the platform. These dots represent the awareness and the will of change of the readers, whose purpose will be to make this article completely disappear once enough people have become aware of the subject. For the development of the visual part we wanted to create something which was a reminding of an editorial style, we developed this web-platform as It could be a spread inside an online magazine. We liked the contrast between the seriousness of using a san-serif font with a humoristic content.


User Experience
The exploration of the platform is simple and intuitive; by clicking on the arrow icons you can easily navigate through the various pages and also the scrolling animations between the pages will help to understand the flow of the platform.


The interactive part are highlighted not only from the color but also from an initial animation which make the interactive elements inside the page bounce for a couple of seconds.
