The Sharing Economy

Issue for the online Magazine Artichoke.



Role Concepet development,

Interaction Designers
Supervisors Leonardo Angelucci
Paolo Jannuzzi

Artichoke is a paper and digital magazine. Artichoke is a project of the students of the Degree Course in Visual Communication and represents a rare and precious emblem of the many activities developed during the courses, in which they were able to merge creativity, passion, choice of contents and their editing in an editorial capacity innovative.

This Issue was meant to investigate the topic of “economy of the future”. As in the last years this reality of crowd-based services is increasing I wanted to approach the topic by doing research on the current sharing economy services and interviewing experts about the "state of art" and possible futures outcomes.

Interface and Interaction modality
The user is actively engaged in the browing of the contemts as in prder to read the different articles he has drag around the the box that are responsables for the navigation. Every box in fact, If clikeded, contains the the different articles.

The idea behinde this kind of navigation is to create a comparison with the sharing economy. The Sharing Economy can't exsist without the contribution and actions of the users an so the articles of this web-page can't be readed without an effort from the readers.
