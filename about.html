— Hello!

I’ve started my studies as a Graphic Designer in 2010, since then I’ve explored in different areas of competence. In 2017 I’ve graduated in Visual Communication at SUPSI (University of Applied Sciences and Arts of Southern Switzerland) with a major in Media & Interaction Design. My Bachelor thesis consisted in the development of an maker kit for teaching electronic to young Egyptian girls, the project was featured in the main Fairs related to Maker Education. As the project received great reviews I then decided to continuing in the development of the project and create a product out of the prototype that I had. This goal brought me to win the scholarship Fondazione Leonardo, which allowed me to further develop the kit and at the same time starting the Master of Advanced Studies in Interaction Design SUPSI, where I will graduate in August 2019.

In between the end of my Bachelor and the start of the Master I did an Internship as Digital Graphic Designer at Martel Innovate (Zurich) where I worked on the communication of several H2020 projects related to digital Innovation and new Technologies. At the time I’ve also worked as assistant of the Master of Advanced Studies in Interaction Design, my role main responsibilities were to taking care of the communications, the development of social media campaign for the recruitment of the student and writing press realised. Since September I am collaborating as a Communication Assistant for research project for the Laboratory in Visual Culture (SUPSI).
