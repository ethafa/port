Ethafa

A game to learn programming and electronics
through playful interactive stories



Role Concepet development, Reseach, Design, Prototyping
Supervisors Serena Cangiano

Ethafa is a game to learn programming and electronics through playful interactive stories and a set of smart characters, the Steammians. By assembling the circuits to animate the Steammias and accessing a tablet application,

young girls and boys from 8 to 12 are engaged into STEAM subjects (Science, Technology, Engineering and Math) and empowered to learn technology through physical and digital interactions.

Ethafa - A game to learn programming and electronics through playful interactive stories from Leyla on Vimeo.

The visual guides
To play with the Steammians, the users have to assemble them by following step by step visual guides.

The guides start with a story accessible through the Ethafa web application, the second key element of the product ecosystem.

The Steammian
The core element of the game ecosystem are the five interactive characters: Eulim, Taquia, Handas, Alryia e Fanin. These characters are called “Steammians”, curious aliens that have different behaviors, each of them reflecting a STEAM subject: Eulim, Taqia, Handas, Alriya, Fanin.



The interactive paper scenario
Following the assembly activity, the users can play with the Steammians by using the interactive paper scenario. The scenario is a paper board representing a fictional city, the Ethafa city. By placing the Steammians on different areas of the scenario, the users can invent stories and program the characters accordingly. When used in the interactive mode, the physical interaction between the character and the scenario can trigger the interactions with a digital application.



The digital application: playing through digital stories
The users can play by accessing a digital application that proposes a series of challenges presented in a story. The first Ethafa stories is called “The virus”: one character gets infected by an informatic virus and its teammates have to work together and solve specific tasks displayed on the application to save him. During the game the users have to collaborate and use problem solving skills and they move the character on the scenario to unlock new sections of the story.
