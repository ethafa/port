Bi-Skills

Installation investing the possibilities of the
soft-skills in the context of an event



Role Concepet development,
Visual Design, Prototyping
Installation
Course Cross-media
Supervisors Andreas Gysing

The installation is a site-specific realized for the event "Terre di Pedemonte" which celebrate the merge of three swiss-towns under the same city, called Terre di Pedemonte. The installation target both people who were involved in this project and the citizens. The project starts from the topic of the soft-skills, as was one of the competences explored during this multidisciplinary project which involved different courses of SUPSI university (Architecture, Visual Communication, Interior Design, Engineering). One of the goal was to create a playful activity whitin the event.

The installation is based on the puzzle-words game where all the words are related to the soft-skills. It's a group activity, the words are hidden under black biscuits placed on top of a black table. The idea is that in order to solve words-puzzle people have to collaborate by strategically eating the biscuits.

Interaction modality
During the event the people were engaged in the game, this activity allowed to start conversations and people stated to coordinate in the eating process. It was interesting to see the how they approach the installation,

at first they weren't sure If the buiscuits were meant to be eaten or not (As they weren't really looking like buiscuits) and after they removed the first biscuts and some of the letters uncoverd people started to understand how it was working.
